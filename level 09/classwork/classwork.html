the battle of didgori

The Battle of Didgori (Georgian: დიდგორის ბრძოლა, romanized: didgoris brdzola).

was fought between the armies of the Kingdom of Georgia and the Seljuk Empire at

the narrow place of Didgori, 40 km west of Tbilisi, on August 12, 1121. The large

Muslim army, under the command of Ilghazi, was unable to maneuver and suffered a

devastating defeat due to King David IV of Georgia's effective military tactics.
